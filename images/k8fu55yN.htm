Digital Verification Solutions for Documents, ID Cards, Labels & Consumer Products

Every year counterfeiting costs billions of dollars to governments and manufacturers worldwide. We have demonstrated our turnkey approach to anti-counterfeit security by embedding digital verification solutions across our solution portfolio including Documents, ID Cards, Labels & Product Packaging.

Depending on the client’s application, our solutions are powered by Authentific or Brand Binary providing instant digital verification methods via WhatsApp, QR-Codes, NFC Tags, Web & SMS.
